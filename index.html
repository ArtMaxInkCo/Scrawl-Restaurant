History of restoraunt

Scrawl restaurant is one of the oldest restaurants the world. It is located opposite the picturesque Seaside Park in Somewhere. This restaurant has long been a symbol of the resort city. Story most famous restaurant started over a century ago. In 1902, the founder of the MMMM resort prince Alexander of Someones brought to Somewhere a disassembled wooden building with clock, which he bought at the world exhibition in the capital of France. Also brought from Paris building for Palace of Princess Eugenia of Someones, which, unfortunately, has not survived to this day. Both buildings together constituted a single architectural ensemble.
In Somewhere, the building was assembled, and without a single nail, and in January 1903 it the grand opening of the first on the Caucasian coast of the Somewhere climatic resort. Throughout history, Emperor Someone II, Stalin, a lot of writers performed on the stage of the restaurant. Emperor Someone II visited Somewhere in 1912 at the insistence of the Prince of Someone. In the house (current restaurant Scrawl) a tea party was held, to which representatives of the Somewhere nobility.
The appearance and interior decoration of the restaurant managed to be preserved to this day almost without changes, despite numerous reconstructions of the structure. The Scrawl restaurant is one of few surviving architectural monuments of the early twentieth century in Somewhere, fully conveying atmosphere of the era. The restaurant continues to operate today. Everything works here too. mechanical watch wound by hand. The Scrawl restaurant in Somewhere hosted the shooting of the famous movie "Winter Evening in Somewhere", which became a classic almost immediately after the release. Writer Someone describes the restaurant in his short story "A Change in Lifestyle".
Today Scrawl is one of the most popular restaurants in Somewhere. With its unique look chic interior and top-level service, it surpasses numerous street Cafe. Incredibly delicious dishes of national cuisine, which are served to the guests of the restaurant, will not leave indifferent even to a sophisticated gourmet. From the windows of the restaurant offers a magnificent view of the a park. AT Somewhere dance groups regularly perform in the restaurant.
