History of restoraunt

Scrawl restaurant is one of the oldest restaurants in Abkhazia. It is located opposite the picturesque Seaside Park in Staraya Gagra. This restaurant has long been a symbol of the resort city. Story most famous restaurant started over a century ago. In 1902, the founder of the Gagra resort prince Alexander of Oldenburg brought to Abkhazia a disassembled wooden building with clock, which he bought at the world exhibition in the capital of France. Also brought from Paris building for Palace of Princess Eugenia of Oldenburg, which, unfortunately, has not survived to this day. Both buildings together constituted a single architectural ensemble.
In Abkhazia, the building was assembled, and without a single nail, and in January 1903 it the grand opening of the first on the Caucasian coast of the Gagra climatic resort. Throughout history, Emperor Nicholas II, Stalin, writers Anton Chekhov Ivan Bunin, Maxim Gorky. Fyodor Chaliapin performed on the stage of the restaurant. Emperor Nicholas II visited Abkhazia in 1912 at the insistence of the Prince of Oldenburg. In the house (current restaurant Scrawl) a tea party was held, to which representatives of the Abkhaz nobility.
The appearance and interior decoration of the restaurant managed to be preserved to this day almost without changes, despite numerous reconstructions of the structure. The Scrawl restaurant is one of few surviving architectural monuments of the early twentieth century in Abkhazia, fully conveying atmosphere of the era. The restaurant continues to operate today. Everything works here too. mechanical watch wound by hand. The Scrawl restaurant in Gagra hosted the shooting of the famous movie "Winter Evening in Gagra", which became a classic almost immediately after the release. Russian writer Vasily Aksyonov describes the restaurant in his short story "A Change in Lifestyle".
Today Scrawl is one of the most popular restaurants in Gagra. With its unique look chic interior and top-level service, it surpasses numerous street Cafe. Incredibly delicious dishes of national cuisine, which are served to the guests of the restaurant, will not leave indifferent even to a sophisticated gourmet. From the windows of the restaurant offers a magnificent view of the a park. AT Abkhaz dance groups regularly perform in the restaurant.
